01 — Ambition

30%

Target the work that should not stay manual.

Our practical ambition is to help automate at least 30% of suitable daily tasks—not with a blanket promise, but by finding repeatable work where AI can create measurable value.

Suitable work depends on process stability, data access, risk, and the role of human judgment.

02 — The method

We start with how work actually happens.

No preselected tool. No automation for its own sake. The first job is to understand where time, context, and decisions are getting lost.

  1. 01

    Interview the people closest to the work

    We listen for repeated tasks, handoff delays, information gaps, and the decisions that still need human expertise.

    Output / workflow evidence
  2. 02

    Pinpoint and prioritize the opportunity

    We assess value, feasibility, data readiness, risk, and adoption before recommending what should be automated.

    Output / opportunity map
  3. 03

    Build the smallest useful solution

    An AI agent, an automation workflow, or a focused product—designed around the real process rather than a generic demo.

    Output / working solution
  4. 04

    Deploy, train, and measure

    We introduce human checkpoints, help the team adopt the new flow, and measure whether it improves the work.

    Output / measured change
