# Booster AI Website Brief

## User request
Build a high-quality website for Booster AI using the Website Builder Loop. Research the market first, choose the strongest style and implementation approach, build and test locally, then ask before GitHub publication.

## Audience
- Business/category: AI consulting, workflow automation, and AI-agent delivery
- Primary audience: business owners, operations leaders, and transformation teams
- Geography/market: Saudi Arabia / GCC is an informed working assumption; the copy remains broadly suitable for English-speaking business audiences
- What they need: a credible way to identify operational friction and turn it into practical AI improvements

## Goal
- Primary goal: generate qualified discovery conversations
- Primary CTA: begin an AI opportunity assessment / discovery session
- Secondary goal: explain the method and distinguish Booster AI from generic AI consulting

## Supplied positioning
Booster AI identifies client pain points and opportunities through consultation and structured interviews, then recommends and delivers practical AI solutions such as AI agents and automation workflows. The stated ambition is to help clients automate at least 30% of suitable daily tasks where practical.

## Content requirements
- Clear statement of who Booster AI helps and what it changes
- Consultation and interview-led discovery
- Pain-point and workflow mapping
- AI-agent and automation delivery
- 30% automation ambition framed as a target, not a guarantee
- Practical process from discovery through measurement
- Honest conversion path without invented contact details

## Brand direction
- Name: Booster AI
- Personality: incisive, energetic, practical, credible, human
- Avoid: generic purple AI gradients, fake dashboards, robots/brains, invented client logos, testimonials, metrics, certifications, or case studies
- Language: English-first; structure and layout should remain Arabic/RTL-ready for a future localized version

## Assets
No logo, photography, client marks, testimonials, contact email, booking link, or finalized Arabic copy were supplied. Use a typographic wordmark and an original CSS-based visual system. Any contact integration remains clearly marked as a launch dependency.

## Constraints
- No API is required for the first local version
- No image-generation API is configured; strong typography and CSS composition must carry the design
- Prefer the smallest suitable static stack
- Respect reduced-motion preferences
- Do not publish until the user explicitly approves publication

## Success criteria
- Visitor understands the offer and method within the first screen
- The 30% target is credible and carefully qualified
- Primary path leads naturally to a discovery conversation
- Site feels original and premium, not like a generic AI template
- Mobile and desktop layouts are verified
- No broken links/assets or console errors
